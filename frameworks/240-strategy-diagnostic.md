# 240+ Strategy Diagnostic Tool

**SUMMARY:** Diagnostic framework containing 240+ cash strategies filtered across 20 categorical buckets.
**Selection Logic:** Driven by current business conditions, root-cause impact, and implementation timeline.
**INFERENCE:** Strategy selection is situational, not prescriptive. Owner control is one factor among 20+ situational variables.
**Deployment:** Used to move clients from symptom-chasing to root-cause cash strategy.
